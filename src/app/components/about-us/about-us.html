Who We Are

Born from a Vision of Enduring Beauty

"Luxury is not about price — it is about the feeling a garment gives you the moment you slip it on."

Roselle was founded with a single conviction: that every woman deserves to feel extraordinary in her everyday life. We are a modern luxury fashion house headquartered in Alexandria, Egypt — a city where Mediterranean grace and ancient grandeur have always coexisted.

Our ateliers source only the finest fabrics — French silks, Italian wools, hand-embroidered Egyptian cotton — and every piece passes through the hands of master tailors who bring decades of expertise to each stitch.
